Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a custom QR code?

Simply tap on the QR code displayed on your lock screen to open the editor. Enter your desired information (URL, contact details, text, etc.) and your QR code will update instantly.

How do I change the background?

Swipe left or right on the background to cycle through our beautiful gradient options. You can also tap the Edit button for more customization options.

What's included in Premium?

Premium users can remove the QuRe branding QR code and unlock additional customization features.

Can I save my customized screen?

Yes! Use the screenshot feature to capture your lock screen setup and share it or save it to your photo library.

Does QuRe work offline?

Yes! Once you've created your QR code, QuRe works completely offline. No internet connection required.
